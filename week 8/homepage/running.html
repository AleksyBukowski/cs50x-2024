My running journey:

I always hated running. Aaaalways. In primary school, my 60m sprint time was quite good - but besides that, I hated running with passion.
One day my uncle told me that I'd be a good runner since i cycle so much - at first, I didn't think so, but i remembered these words. A few months later, I started running - I had to push myself through the first 2 weeks because of how bad the experience was - I could barely run 500 meters without stopping! After doing that, I actually started to like running and it became my second favourite hobby after cycling - thanks uncle!
