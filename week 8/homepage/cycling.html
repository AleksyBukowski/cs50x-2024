My cycling journey:

I started cycling a lot around 2019 due to stress - I found cycling to be a great way to relieve it. At first, I couldn't cycle up a little hill near my hometown - I nearly had a heart attack once!
After a while, I found out that i actually love cycling - once I've done my first 100KM ride, there was no going back anymore. 4 years later I managed to ride all the way from my small hometown to the capital of Poland - Warsaw, and it took me around 18 hours and 350 kilometers!
I'm definately not planning to stop anytime soon.
